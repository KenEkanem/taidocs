Welcome to Trust and Identity Solutions

Trust and Identity Solutions Limited is a pioneering provider of identity verification and compliance solutions in Nigeria. We leverage the National Identification Number (NIN) to create secure, compliant digital identity ecosystems for businesses, government agencies, and individuals.

From workforce vetting to regulatory compliance, secure digital identities, and risk management solutions, we are committed to co-creating impactful solutions that address critical challenges and advance societal and organizational goals.
